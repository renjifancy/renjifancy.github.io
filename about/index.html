About Me

I am a doctor at Department of Emergency, Renji Hospital, School of Medicine, Shanghai JiaoTong University. I received my M.M. degree in Tongji University, under the supervision of Prof. Xu jiahong.Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in Second Military Medical University.

I focus on sepsis and acute lung injury, and am committed to becoming a clinical scientist as well as bioinformatics engineer.
